04 // Rules And Prep

The stuff that saves the day.

Useful details that should stay visible instead of getting buried.

Michigan trip rules
  • Anglers age 17 and up need a valid Michigan fishing license.
  • For short trips, the 24-hour nonresident option is usually the cleanest move.
  • Daily salmonid limits and species-specific rules can change, so confirm final limits before travel.
  • Most charter crews allow moderate beer or wine, but hard liquor is commonly prohibited.
Pack like a pro
  • Polarized sunglasses, a non-cotton layer, and soft-soled shoes with grip.
  • Small cooler for transport after fish cleaning if the captain does not provide storage bags.
  • Motion-sickness plan before the boat leaves, not after the lake turns on you.
  • Cash for mate tip, launch snacks, and any cook-your-catch fee.
